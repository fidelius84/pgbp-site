Transparency & On-Chain Verification

PGBP is committed to transparency. All token supply and transfers are publicly verifiable on the Ethereum blockchain. This page provides the key information needed to independently verify the PGBP token.

On-Chain Verification

Anyone can verify the following on Etherscan:

  • Total supply: View token on Etherscan
  • Contract source code: Verified and publicly readable on Etherscan
  • Treasury balance: View treasury wallet
  • Burn/redemption transactions: View redemption wallet
