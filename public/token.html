Issuance & Redemption

  • Model: Issuer-only OTC. No exchange listings or third-party liquidity guarantees.
  • Purchase fee: 5%
  • Redemption fee: 0% (issuer covers bank fees)
  • Payment rails: Faster Payments (UK), CHAPS, SWIFT

Cut-off Times (London Time)

  • Purchases submitted by 15:00 receive same-day tokens.
  • Redemptions up to £100k by 15:00 receive same-day payout.
  • Redemptions £100k–£1m settle T+1.
  • Redemptions over £1m settle T+2.

Compliance

KYC/AML verification is required for all purchases and redemptions. US persons are permitted with enhanced due diligence (EDD). Sanctioned countries are excluded. Travel Rule compliance applies.
