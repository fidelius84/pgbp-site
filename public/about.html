About Pegged GBP (PGBP)

PGBP is a GBP-referenced stablecoin built on the Ethereum blockchain as an ERC-20 token. It is designed to provide a stable, on-chain representation of British Pound Sterling.

What is PGBP?

Pegged GBP (PGBP) enables holders to transact in a digital token that references the value of GBP. The token has a fixed supply of 300,000,000 units minted at deployment. No additional tokens can ever be created — minting is permanently disabled and the smart contract is immutable.

Key Features

  • Fixed supply: 300,000,000 PGBP tokens, set at deployment
  • Minting disabled: No new tokens can be created
  • Burn enabled: Tokens can be permanently removed from supply
  • Immutable contract: The smart contract cannot be upgraded or modified
  • Source verified: Contract source code is verified on Etherscan
  • Network: Ethereum (ERC-20)

Issuer

PGBP is issued by PGBP PAYMENTS LTD (Company No. 16925298), a UK-registered entity. The issuer handles all primary issuance and redemptions directly — there are no exchange listings or third-party liquidity guarantees.

How It Works

  • Purchase: Buyers submit a request, complete KYC/AML compliance checks, and receive PGBP tokens after GBP payment.
  • Redemption: Holders schedule a redemption slot, and GBP is paid out via bank transfer (Faster Payments, CHAPS, or SWIFT).
  • Fees: Purchase fee is 5%. Redemption is free — the issuer covers bank transfer fees.

Compliance

All transactions require KYC/AML verification. US persons are permitted with enhanced due diligence (EDD). Sanctioned countries are excluded. Travel Rule compliance applies.
